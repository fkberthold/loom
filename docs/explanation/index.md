# Explanation

Explanation is the *acquisition + cognition* quadrant: the wider,
reflective view. Explanation answers *why this and not that?* — it
admits opinion, weighs alternatives, and connects loom's design
choices to the constraints they were chosen against.

> **What explanation is not.** It does not give direct instruction
> (that's [How-to](../how-to/index.md)) and does not catalogue
> surface (that's [Reference](../reference/index.md)). If you find
> yourself reaching for code-by-code procedure, you've drifted out
> of this quadrant.
